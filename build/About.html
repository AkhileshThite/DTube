About

EtherTube is the one of the first decentralized applications I've designed for the Ethereum blockchain. It was built using React, Truffle and Ganache.

Problem EtherTube solves:

• Storing any media on the blockchain is so costly, hence EtherTube solves this problem by storing IPFS video hashes to the blockchain.
• On EtherTube Nobody can take down your video.
• It's green as compared to the energy consumption of centralized servers.
• It's ad-free! Now you can watch videos without those annoying ads, this platform treats you as a "user" not the "product”.
